ABOUT ME

My name is KD, and I am currently pursuing a BCA course from MSI. I have been an anime enthusiast since the 6th grade. This web development project of mine is a platform where I share my favorite animes and movies. By connecting with me through this website, I can provide you with recommendations to kickstart your anime viewing journey.

FAVOURITE ANIME

One Piece, my favorite anime, is an epic journey of friendship, adventure, and battle. Its unique characters, each with their own dreams, navigate the vast seas in search of the ultimate treasure, One Piece. The thrilling plot and profound themes make it a timeless masterpiece.

FAVOURITE MOVIE

A Silent Voice, my favourite movie, is a poignant exploration of redemption, friendship, and empathy. It beautifully portrays the journey of a boy seeking forgiveness from a deaf girl he once bullied. Its emotional depth and stunning animation make it a cinematic masterpiece.

FAVOURITE CHARACTER

Houtaro Oreki, my favourite character from Hyouka, is a high school student known for his energy-saving lifestyle. His logical thinking and deductive skills, despite his laid-back attitude, make him a captivating character. His transformation throughout the series adds depth, making him truly memorable.

BEST MOVIES OF ALL TIME

YOUR NAME

In this enchanting anime film directed by Makoto Shinkai, a high school runaway, Hodaka, befriends Hina, a girl with the power to control the weather. As Tokyo faces incessant rain, their bond challenges the forces of nature and explores the cost of altering fate for love and survival.

WEATHERING WITH YOU

Directed by Makoto Shinkai, "Your Name" follows Taki and Mitsuha, two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies and lives. As they navigate their intertwined destinies, they strive to unravel the cosmic connection binding them and prevent a catastrophic event, blending romance, fantasy, and breathtaking animation.

A SILENT VOICE

Naoko Yamada directs this emotionally charged anime, adapted from Yoshitoki ÅŒima's manga. "A Silent Voice" delves into the repercussions of bullying as Shoya Ishida seeks redemption for tormenting Shoko Nishimiya, a deaf classmate. The film poignantly explores themes of forgiveness, redemption, and the power of genuine human connections.

BEST ANIMES OF ALL TIME

DEATH NOTE

"Death Note" revolves around Light Yagami who discovers a mysterious notebook granting him the ability to kill anyone by writing their name. The series explores themes of morality and justice as Light becomes a vigilante, pursued by the enigmatic detective L.

BERSERK

"Berserk" follows the lone mercenary Guts in a dark medieval world plagued by demons and political intrigue. Guts battles both inner demons and external foes, with a grim tone and intense, visceral storytelling.

MONSTER

"Monster" centers on Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a surgeon who saves a young boy, only to discover the child becomes a psychopathic killer. Tenma's pursuit of justice reveals a complex conspiracy, questioning morality and the consequences of one's actions.
